Arguments for PTHREADS version

    graph filename
    nodes
    threshold
    d
    threads

Example:
./pagerank_pthreads wed_Google.txt 916428 0.0001 0.85 8


Arguments for serial version

    graph filename
    nodes
    threshold
    d

Example:
./pagerank_serial web-Google.txt 916428 0.0001 0.85
